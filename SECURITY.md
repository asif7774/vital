## Reporting a Vulnerability

We take security seriously. If you discover a vulnerability, please do NOT report it as a public issue.

Instead, please send an email to **asif.ansari7774@gmail.com**.

Please include:
*   A description of the vulnerability.
*   Steps to reproduce.
*   The potential impact.

We will acknowledge your report within 48 hours and will provide an update on the fix timeline.

Thank you for helping keep Vital safe!
